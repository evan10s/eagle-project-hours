Eagle Project Hours Calculator

This calculator makes it easy to generate a report for the hours worked for all of your Eagle Scout project workdays.

Start by entering in information about who went to each of your workdays below.

Also, it really doesn't matter how you enter the times. If you leave off the AM or PM, the calculator will infer the AM/PM and add it to the field for you.

For start times, times with an hour from 6-11 will become 6 AM, 7 AM, etc. If this behavior doesn't match your times (for example, you had workdays start at 6 PM or later)

For end times, times with an hours from 1-7 will become 1 PM, 2 PM, etc. As with start times, you can change this behavior.

If you enter the AM/PM on a time, the value will be reformatted slightly, but the AM/PM will not change.
